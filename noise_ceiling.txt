- Signal Reliability and Upper-Bound Analysis
Due to the naturalistic, single-pass continuous viewing paradigm of the Algonauts 2025 Friends dataset, traditional within-subject noise ceilings (which require repeated presentations of identical stimuli) could not be computed. Instead, we estimated the theoretical upper bound of model performance using Inter-Subject Correlation (ISC). ISC quantifies the shared, stimulus-driven variance across different subjects watching the same sequence, treating inter-subject shared variance as the maximum explorable signal (Nastase et al., 2019). The pairwise ISC across our test clips serves as our proxy noise ceiling.
(Citation to include: Nastase, S. A., Gazzola, V., Hasson, U., & Keysers, C. (2019). Measuring shared responses across subjects using intersubject correlation. Social Cognitive and Affective Neuroscience, 14(6), 667-685.)
